Dr. Sin-gyu Hyun

현신규 박사

(1911~1986)

Dr. Hyun is botanist who restored the ruined South Korea Forest because of 3 years Korean-War on 1950.

전쟁후 황페한 산하를 녹화하고자 리기다 소나무, 은사시포플라 나무등 추위에 강하고 빨리자라는 수목을 개발.


  • 1911 Born in north part of Korea.
  • 1936 Graduated from the Department of Forestry, Kyushu Imperial University, Japan.
  • 1949 Doctor of Agriculture, Kyushu Imperial University, Japan.

  • 1945 Suwon Agriculture and Forestry School Assistant Professor.
  • 1945 Chief of the Forestry Experiment Station of the U.S. Military Administration.
  • 1946 Professor at Suwon Agricultural and Forestry College.
  • 1960 The first president of the Korean Forestry Society.
  • 1963 3years served Director of the Rural Development Administration.
  • 1965 12years served Professor, College of Agriculture, Seoul National University.
  • 1949 he was the first Korean to receive a doctorate in forestry.

  • 1951 the American Forest Genetic Research Institute conducted a study on forest breeding.
  • 1952 He returned to Korea and developed the Rigiteda Pine. He also worked hard to improve poplar, a variety suitable for forest greening because of its rapid growth rate.

His tireless enthusiasm for his studies and his sense of mission for greening the homeland have become the foundation for today's green Korea. Dr. Shin-kyu Hyun, who devoted his life to forestry for the country. His name will be remembered for a long time with the honor of rebuilding the environment with science and technology.
